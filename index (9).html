Advocate Kanti Prasad Maithani

Senior Advocate — Pauri Garhwal, Uttarakhand

A name synonymous with justice in the hills of Garhwal. Advocate Kanti Prasad Maithani is not just a lawyer — he is a pillar of trust that thousands of families across Pauri Garhwal have relied upon for more than 38 years.

Born and raised in the heart of Uttarakhand, Advocate Maithani began his legal practice at the District Court of Pauri in the early days of his career and has since built an extraordinary reputation across all courts of Garhwal. His command over civil disputes, criminal defense, property and land matters, family law, motor accident claims, consumer protection, and land acquisition cases is widely recognized by both the legal fraternity and the communities he serves.

What truly sets him apart is his deep-rooted connection to the people of Garhwal. He understands the unique challenges of land ownership in the hills — from complex revenue matters to civil, criminal, family, consumer, motor accident, and land acquisition cases — with a clarity that no outsider can match. Advocate Maithani is known for fighting tirelessly for his clients, whether they are a farmer contesting a land grab or a family seeking justice in a criminal case.

Over the decades, he has handled approximately 5,000 cases with a remarkable success rate, earning the respect of judges, peers, and clients alike. His commitment goes beyond the courtroom — he is often seen counseling families, guiding first-time litigants through the intimidating legal process, and ensuring that justice remains accessible to everyone, regardless of their financial background.

For the people of Pauri Garhwal, Advocate Kanti Prasad Maithani is more than a legal counsel. He is a guardian of their rights, a voice for the voiceless, and a name that carries the weight of three decades of unwavering service to justice.
